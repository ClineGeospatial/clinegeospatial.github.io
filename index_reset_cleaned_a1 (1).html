Project Highlight: Crash Geocoding for NC Safety Analysis

In support of North Carolina‚Äôs traffic safety initiatives, Cline Geospatial provided manual crash geocoding services to improve the positional accuracy of crash records. By aligning crash reports with real-world road network data and field-verified mile markers, our work enabled precise identification of high-crash corridors. These insights directly supported NCDOT and Vision Zero planning teams in prioritizing locations for safety improvements such as signal modifications, lane redesigns, and pedestrian enhancements.

This project illustrates the impact of detailed GIS QA/QC workflows and manual review in transportation planning. Through precise spatial correction, we improved the analytical foundation for corridor safety scoring, crash clustering, and project funding justification.
