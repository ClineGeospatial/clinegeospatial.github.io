About Cline Geospatial

Cline Geospatial is a woman-owned GIS consulting firm with a proven record of delivering spatial solutions across government and industry. Our team brings deep experience supporting federal agencies, managing state-level transportation initiatives, and winning competitive municipal contracts.

We specialize in GIS web application development, spatial analysis, and cartographic mapping—tailoring every project to meet client goals with precision, clarity, and insight. From designing mobile-ready dashboards to conducting complex geospatial modeling, we make location data meaningful.

Whether you're a small town seeking crash mapping support or a state DOT deploying enterprise workflows, no contract is too big or too small. We pride ourselves on flexibility, technical rigor, and a commitment to turning geospatial data into smarter decisions.
