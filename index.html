Tax Parcel Impact Analysis App

This interactive web mapping app enables transportation planners to draw road alignments and evaluate impacted tax parcels. The app supports spatial and attribute filtering, empowering users to analyze property value, structure counts, and local land attribution. It demonstrates how a standardized tax strategy can support regional transportation infrastructure planning.
