Modeling & Analytics

Flood Risk Modeling – Culmback Dam, Sultan WA
ArcGIS Pro + lidar-derived DTM hydrology modeling. Topography-based flow analysis confirmed Sultan, WA, is largely protected from dam breach flooding.

Redistricting & Demographic Analysis – Illinois
ArcGIS Insights dashboards analyzed racial impacts of redistricting plans. Choropleths and bar charts revealed boundary effects on minority groups.

Programming

Custom Python Tool – Parsing Mass Violence Events
ArcPy tool parsed mass shooting events into decade-based shapefiles, exported ArcGIS layouts and dashboards for trend analysis. Automation enabled efficient and repeatable workflows.

Database Management

National Campgrounds App – Wyoming
PostgreSQL/PostGIS database supports an interactive campground filter map, integrating federal datasets and optimized with spatial indexing.

Wildlife Data Editing – GW Parkway
ArcGIS Enterprise SDE + versioned feature services allow live editing of wildlife records. Attribute rules, constraints, and mobile sync support field edits.

Web Services
